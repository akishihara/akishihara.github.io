Visiting ISS

Application for a Research/Visiting Student

  • I do not accept any student as a research fellow (研究生 in Japanese). If you are seriously interested in my supervision, please enrol in the graduate programme of the graduate school of economics and fulfill the requirement in the first year (see also the guidance).
  • Provided that you are a graduate (doctoral) student in another research institute and hope to visit the ISS by my host, I accept you as a visiting student of the ISS only if
    1. I know you well in person, or
    2. you have a reference letter, written by a scholar whom I know in person, which strongly supports your visit in the ISS.
    These are just necessary conditions and do not necessarily guarantee your visit.
  • Please do not email or make any contact to me for inquiry unless the conditions above are satisfied. I shall not respond to any message on this matter.
